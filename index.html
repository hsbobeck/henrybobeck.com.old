About Me

I am a web developer/designer living in the United States. Currently, I am spending my free time learning the ins and outs of web development and doing my best to improve my skills in each of these fields. I enjoy the satisfaction of taking an idea from my imagination and using code to transform that mental image into something tangible online.

Other than web development/design, I enjoy playing games, editing videos, creating electronic music, and more. I am very interested in other branches of computer science such as software programming and video game development, and have an interest in both the technical and visual aspects of these areas.

Lately I have been making my way through the curriculum on freeCodeCamp.com and have been using this as a way to get a good starting point to learn to learn about web development and programming in general. So far it has been very helpful and I can't help but recommend it to others looking to learn.
